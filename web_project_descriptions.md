## 1. AirbillsPay - Fintech Platform

**Role:** Full Stack Developer (Next.js & TypeScript)

**Project Overview**
Paying for simple utilities shouldn't be a part-time job. Users were juggling multiple glitchy apps just to buy airtime or pay for TV, leading to high frustration and dropout rates. I built a unified, high-performance platform that turned a chaotic 10-minute chore into a seamless 30-second experience.

**The Challenge**

- Users had to switch between different apps for different bills, causing friction.
- Existing solutions were unreliable, leading to failed transactions and lost trust.
- High user churn due to poor UX and slow performance.

**The Solution**
I engineered a centralized platform using Next.js and PostgreSQL. I focused heavily on speed and reliability, creating a "one-tap" experience for payments. By simplifying the user journey and optimizing the backend for real-time processing, we removed every barrier between the user and their goal.

**Key Results**

- **Transaction time reduced from 10 minutes to under 30 seconds.**
- **User retention increased significantly** as trust in the platform grew.
- **Captured a new segment of users** who had previously abandoned digital payments.

## 2. Kayi Digital Bank - Neobank & Super App

**Role:** Lead Frontend Engineer

**Project Overview**
The vision was ambitious: a digital bank for Nigeria's unbanked and the global diaspora. The problem? They had zero infrastructure to make it happen. I helped build the entire digital banking MVP from the ground up, delivering a secure super-app that handles payments, wallets, and international remittances.

**The Challenge**

- Complex requirements combining local banking with international remittances.
- Security and trust were non-negotiable for a financial product.
- Needed to launch quickly to capture market share without compromising quality.

**The Solution**
I led the frontend development using Next.js and TypeScript, ensuring bank-grade security with a consumer-friendly interface. We built a robust "super app" architecture that could scale with millions of users. The focus was on making complex financial operations feel simple and safe for the end-user.

**Key Results**

- **Successfully flagged off and launched** a fully regulated digital bank MVP.
- **Unified multiple financial services** (payments, wallets, remittances) into one app.
- **Enabled secure cross-border transactions** for the diaspora market.

## 3. SaaS Analytics Dashboard

**Role:** Frontend Developer

**Project Overview**
Data is useless if you can't understand it. The founder was drowning in complex Google Analytics charts, paralyzed by information overload. I built a custom, focused dashboard that surfaced only the 5 metrics that actually mattered to their business revenue.

**The Challenge**

- Founder was avoiding analytics because the existing tools were too complex.
- Critical business decisions were being made on gut feeling rather than data.
- Wasted hours trying to configure reports instead of acting on insights.

**The Solution**
I created a clean, purpose-built dashboard using Next.js. We stripped away the noise and visualized the core KPIs: revenue, churn, active users, and growth rate. It wasn't about showing _more_ data, it was about showing the _right_ data in a way that compelled action.

**Key Results**

- **Daily active usage of analytics** by the founder (up from "never").
- **Decision-making shifted from guesswork to data-driven** strategies.
- **Saved hours of configuration time** every week.
