## How to Use This Database

### 1. **Research Before Outreach**
- Visit their website
- Check their social media presence
- Understand their current pain points
- Look for signs of manual processes or inefficiencies

### 2. **Personalization**
Use the pitch templates in [PITCH_TEMPLATES.md](PITCH_TEMPLATES.md):
- LinkedIn DM (150-200 words)
- Email Pitch (250-350 words)
- WhatsApp Pitch (100-150 words) - especially for Dubai market

### 3. **Reference Relevant Blog Post**
Each business has a relevant blog post linked. When pitching:
- Mention: "I actually wrote about this exact challenge you're facing..."
- Include the blog post URL
- Position yourself as an expert who understands their industry

### 4. **Track Your Outreach**
Create a simple spreadsheet to track:
- Date contacted
- Method (LinkedIn/Email/WhatsApp)
- Response received?
- Follow-up scheduled?
- Status (Cold/Warm/Hot/Closed)

### 5. **Follow-Up Strategy**
- Day 0: Initial outreach
- Day 3: Follow-up if no response
- Day 7: Final follow-up
- Move on if no response after 3 attempts
