You are building a modern, elegant, and sleek 2-page website for a product builder who helps founders turn ideas into real products quickly.

🎨 **Design Style**
- Clean, minimal, and elegant layout.
- Dark theme with `backgroundColor: #0E0904` and `textColor: #FFF3E8`.
- Use soft shadows, rounded corners, and generous whitespace.
- Font: modern sans-serif (Inter, Sora, or Geist Sans).
- Subtle animations (Framer Motion style) for section entrances and hover states.
- Keep the interface lightweight and performance-optimized.
- Visuals should feel premium, tech-forward, and calm — not flashy.

---

## 🏠 Page 1: Home Page (`/`)

### Purpose:
Introduce the builder and showcase their best product work for founders.

### Layout Sections:

1. **Navbar**
   - Transparent or matching background color.
   - Links: `Home`, `Automations`, `Contact`.
   - Sticky on scroll.

2. **Hero Section**
   - Headline:
     “I help founders turn ideas into real products — fast.”
   - Subheadline:
     “From MVPs to AI-powered automations, I help both non-technical and technical founders build and launch their ideas efficiently.”
   - Two call-to-action buttons:
     - “View My Work”
     - “Explore Automations”
   - Optional small photo or avatar on the side.

3. **Services Section**
   - Title: “What I Build”
   - Two or three cards representing services:
     - **MVP Development:** “I build fullstack web apps that help founders test and scale their ideas fast.”
     - **Automation Workflows:** “I design AI automations that save time, reduce costs, and boost efficiency.”
     - *(Optional)* **UI/UX Design:** “Simple, intuitive interfaces that make products delightful to use.”
   - Include small icons or minimal visuals for each.

4. **Projects Section**
   - Title: “Featured Projects”
   - Responsive grid of 3–6 cards.
   - Each project card shows:
     - Screenshot or thumbnail.
     - Name, short description, tech stack, and link to live/demo.
   - Example categories: “MVPs”, “Web Apps”, “AI Tools”.

5. **About Section**
   - Concise paragraph (3–4 lines):
     “I’m a product builder focused on helping founders bring their ideas to life. I combine strong fullstack development skills with AI automation expertise to build, launch, and scale MVPs fast.”
   - Optionally include a small image or personal photo.

6. **Contact Section**
   - Simple, elegant contact area:
     - Email: `mailto:` link.
     - GitHub, LinkedIn, and Twitter/X icons.
     - Optional contact form (name, email, message).

7. **Footer**
   - Minimal footer with copyright.
   - Navigation links repeated (Home | Automations | Contact).

---

## ⚙️ Page 2: Automations Page (`/automation`)

### Purpose:
Explain and showcase how you help businesses and founders streamline their processes using AI automation workflows.

### Layout Sections:

1. **Hero Section**
   - Title: “Automate Your Workflow with AI”
   - Subtitle: “I design custom automations that save time, reduce costs, and scale productivity — so you can focus on building.”
   - Button: “Book a Call” or “See Case Studies”

2. **Process Section**
   - Title: “How It Works”
   - 3-step flow with icons:
     1. Understand your workflow  
        _We analyze your current processes and identify repetitive tasks._
     2. Design smart automation  
        _We integrate tools like OpenAI, Zapier, and custom APIs to automate efficiently._
     3. Deploy and support  
        _We implement, test, and maintain your automation system._

3. **Showcase Section**
   - Title: “Automation in Action”
   - Grid of case study cards showing examples:
     - “AI-Powered Customer Support Assistant”
     - “Automated Lead Nurturing for SaaS”
     - “Content Generation and Scheduling Tool”
   - Each card includes a short description and tech stack badges.

4. **Tech Stack / Tools**
   - Logos or small icons: OpenAI, Zapier, LangChain, Next.js, Python, etc.
   - Short line of text:
     “I connect modern AI tools with your existing systems to create seamless, intelligent workflows.”

5. **Call-to-Action Section**
   - Large centered heading:
     “Ready to automate your workflow?”
   - Button: “Contact Me” or “Book a Free Consultation”

## 🧩 Technical Notes
- Framework: Next.js 14 (App Router)
- Styling: Tailwind CSS
- Components: ShadCN UI or clean custom components
- Animations: Framer Motion (fade/slide-in effects)
- Fully responsive design (mobile, tablet, desktop)
- Use smooth transitions between pages
- Keep text legible and contrast strong (#FFF3E8 on #0E0904)

---

## 🌐 Tone & Aesthetic
- Confident, professional, and founder-friendly.
- Focus on clarity, trust, and modern design.
- Typography should feel balanced and readable.
- Each section should have consistent spacing and visual rhythm.
- The website should feel calm, premium, and trustworthy.

---

🎯 **Goal Summary**
Build a two-page portfolio website that:
1. Positions the creator as a product builder helping founders turn ideas into MVPs fast.  
2. Highlights both **MVP development** and **AI automation** as core services.  
3. Showcases projects beautifully and drives visitors to connect or collaborate.
