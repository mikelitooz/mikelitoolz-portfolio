2. Yelp: Filter "family-owned"

## USA-Specific Outreach Tips

### 1. **Email is Primary in USA**
Unlike Dubai (WhatsApp-first), USA businesses prefer:
- Professional email (70%)
- Phone calls (20%)
- LinkedIn (10%)

### 2. **Best Contact Times**
- Tuesday-Thursday, 10am-3pm local time
- Avoid Mondays (busy) and Fridays (wind-down)
- December: Contact early in month (before holiday rush)

### 3. **USA Business Culture**
- Direct and to-the-point
- ROI-focused (show numbers)
- Time-sensitive (respect their time)
- Less relationship-building upfront vs. Dubai

### 4. **Compliance**
- CAN-SPAM Act: Include physical address, unsubscribe
- GDPR (if EU customers): Privacy policy required
- State laws: Check local regulations
