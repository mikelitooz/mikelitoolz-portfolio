## Writing Quality Checklist

Each blog post adheres to the master writing framework:

- ✅ **Empathy First:** Opens by reflecting reader's current pain and operational reality
- ✅ **Clarity Over Complexity:** Uses simple language, short sentences, familiar terms
- ✅ **Emotional Resonance:** Evokes hope (automation solves this), fear (competitors are already doing this), achievement (grow without proportional stress)
- ✅ **Psychological Triggers:** Authority (market data), Social proof (successful companies), Scarcity (December window), Clarity (specific ROI calculations)
- ✅ **Flow Formula:** Hook (shocking cost) → Relate (December pain points) → Value (automation solutions) → Action (consultation CTA)
- ✅ **No em dashes:** Replaced with commas or periods for natural flow
- ✅ **Varied sentence structure:** Mix of short punchy sentences and longer flowing explanations
- ✅ **Conversational tone:** "Here's what's actually happening" vs. "One might observe"
- ✅ **Outcome-driven:** Every section guides toward "automation solves this specific problem"
- ✅ **Ends with momentum:** Clear next step that feels emotionally satisfying
