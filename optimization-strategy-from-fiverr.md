Gigs best practices
As a new freelancer on Fiverr, you want to offer the best experience possible for your clients. The following tips, guidelines, and best practices will help you grow and become a top Fiverr freelancer, and achieve the growth you need to manage and build your business online.

Fiverr policies and guidelines
We want to ensure that Fiverr is a high-quality and safe marketplace, which is why all members need to be aware of our policies and guidelines listed in our Community Standards. Here are our main policies and guidelines for both you and your customers to have the best Fiverr experience possible.

Optimizing your Gig's content
We're here to help you take your business to the next level. To create a Gig that truly stands out and captures attention, focusing on your content is key. The right words can attract the right customers—here's what you'll need to optimize yours:

Clean communication is key
To maintain a professional marketplace, the service you're providing should align with our Terms of Service.
All communication regarding your business should be kept on Fiverr. When communicating on Fiverr, you enjoy the benefits of our platform's ease of use, security, and having someone to turn to in case a problem arises.
Be professional when communicating with your customers. Be polite and use courteous language. Send them informative updates and messages of value to maintain a collaborative relationship.
Spamming is not allowed on Fiverr. Please respect our members' privacy by not contacting them with offers, questions, suggestions, or anything not directly related to their Gigs or orders. If you receive a spam message or a message violating our Terms of Service, use the spam or report icon accordingly.
The best way to get a good review is to be professional, responsive, and courteous. Asking customers for positive ratings goes against our terms.
And most importantly, don't discriminate based on gender, religion, ethnicity, or nationality. Diversity is an integral part of Fiverr!

A clever, concise title
This is your first impression. It's what shows up in the search, so incorporate the most relevant keyword in your title so that your service will appear in the search results. Your Gig title starts with, "I will," so make sure a strong verb follows it, and that it is grammatically correct.

Tag yourself
Browse your subcategory to see what the most common keywords are for your service. Then make sure you add the relevant search tags to your Gig.

Describe what you offer
This is the section where you make a case for yourself as a Fiverr freelancer. Be clear about what you offer, the added value you bring to your service, your skills, and what distinguishes you from others in your field. This is also where you should mention the type of service you aren't willing to do. Avoid repeating information that you've provided in other sections of your Gig.
When relevant, specify what languages, platforms, and software you will use. Don't forget to include keywords that your target customers will look for. Mention your turnaround time, and the number of revisions you will provide as well.

Give them all the answers
To ensure it's a perfect match between you and a customer, include FAQs about your services. This way, there's no confusion as to what you will deliver. Include all the questions you usually get asked when delivering a service and any other relevant information you feel your client should know before ordering.

Use a template in your Gig requirements
To save time, set expectations, and avoid misunderstandings: lay out all your requirements so your customers can specify exactly what they need. Make sure your requirements cover everything you need to start working on the order.

Optimizing your Gig's gallery
Create and use visually pleasing Gig images. We are judgmental by nature and when potential clients come across your Gig, your Gig’s level of appeal is determined very quickly. If judged as “attractive,” your CTR is likely to be higher and clients are more likely to spend more time reviewing your service. Overall, a product or service’s online representation influences potential clients’ perceived usefulness or enjoyment. Generate more revenue and attract high-quality clients for your business by using Gig images that stand out. Make the most of how your business presents itself in the marketplace using our Gig image guidelines.

Video is also a great way to showcase your skills. Flaunt your expertise by uploading a showreel with a variety of reviews with files. Keep in mind that Gigs with videos perform better.

For non-visual services, create a unique and relevant image that explains your Gig and attracts customers. Tip: To add credibility to your Gig, we recommend including a photo of yourself in your Gig image. Once your image is uploaded, review your Gig card to ensure your image is centered and looks good!

As mentioned above, we recommend including videos. You can include a web/app preview, or a video of yourself explaining what you bring to the table. You can upload a PDF with your resume and more reviews with files, as they are typically the best option for displaying written-word content.
